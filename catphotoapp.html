Recipe for cake

This delicious homemade lasagna is layered with rich meat sauce, creamy béchamel, and melted cheese, making it the perfect comfort food for any occasion.

Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Tomato sauce

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a large pan, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add chopped onions and garlic, and sauté until soft and fragrant.
  3. Add the ground beef to the pan and cook until browned. Break up the meat with a spoon as it cooks.
  4. Stir in the tomato sauce, tomato paste, dried oregano, dried basil, salt, and black pepper. Let the sauce simmer for 15-20 minutes, stirring occasionally.
